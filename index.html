We are Beetfabrik

Our aim is to spread the word on how amazing beets are, as well as serve everyone delicious food, stand for animal welfare, and revolutionise the hospitality sector.

"What? Like it's hard?"
- Elle Woods

organic & guilt-free

We believe every being deserves a happy life full of respect and dignity. Which is why we've adopted cows and goats that were rescued from factory farming, and we've given them a home. Every dairy product that is used in our kitchen, and that you can buy in our online shop, comes directly from our farm. We also, obviously, grow our own beets, and we source our other produce locally.

Our Story

Founded in East London in 2003 by two best friends through their equal love for beets, we have since opened three restaurants, and we run our own beet and livestock farm just outside of London. We're not quite vegan - but all our dairy products were made from our very own rescue goats and cows who live on the farm as well. (By the way: did you know we also offer farm tours?)

Work For Us

Beetfabrik was founded by industry professionals who wanted to create a safe and fair work environment. We understand that hospitality workers generally work long hours, yet are massively underpaid for their work. That's why we pay *actual* competitive wages, alongside many great benefits like accessible therapy sessions, discounted gym memberships, and free meals when you're at work.

We wanted to see a change in the industry, so we made it ourselves. If you'd like to be part of the revolution, apply now.
